How to Add the Addon to Blender

  1. Open Blender and go to Edit > Preferences.
  2. In the Preferences window, click on the "Add-ons" tab.
  3. Click the "Install" button at the top of the window.
  4. Navigate to the location where you saved the downloaded "samething2.py" file.
  5. Select the file and click "Install Add-on".
  6. Find the "Offline Text to 3D Models" addon in the list (you can use the search bar).
  7. Enable the addon by clicking the checkbox next to its name.
  8. Close the Preferences window.
  9. The addon should now be available for use in Blender!
